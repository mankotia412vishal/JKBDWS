JKBD&WS Recent Camp

"JKBD&WS" had a truly magnificent day at its 32nd blood donation camp on 5th February 2023 at the Ind-Pak International Border in Suchetgarh, Rs Pura. The camp was a great success, with over 100 donors registering to give blood and an impressive 70 actually donating. Despite some unfortunate setbacks, such as inclement weather and limited time, the turnout was fantastic and demonstrated the incredible support Jkbdws continues to receive from the community.

The dedication of this camp to the memory of Sepoy Sukhjinder Singh made it all the more poignant, and it was truly heartening to see so many people come together to pay tribute to our brave soldiers. The generosity of those who donated blood will make a real difference in the lives of those in need, and the support of those who were unable to donate due to circumstances beyond their control was greatly appreciated.

This camp was yet another testament to the selflessness and kindness of the human spirit, and Jkbdws is incredibly proud to have played a part in bringing people together for this noble cause. We would like to extend our heartfelt thanks to all who participated and made this camp such a resounding success. We hope that this positive momentum will continue, and that we can make a real difference in the lives of those in need.
