About Us

Food and other essential things are the basic requirements of the life but some families in the India are not be able get food to live their life and they will also have not much will power to earn their bread and the butter. So our "Sanastha" is working in this field to provide the bread and butter to poor people.Our "Sanastha" is working for poor girl marriage, mainly to perform the “adopt” of daughters whose families can't afford a wedding. We try to support the girl and her family by bearing the expenses of the wedding and demolish dowry and suicide due to lack of funds for marriage. has passed by.Moreover we are also working in the field for old age persons whom had no source of income we are providing the shetler and food and provide the essential support which they needed. we are also working in donating the blood There is a constant need for a regular supply of blood because it can be stored only for a limited period of time before use. so if any family facing any difficulties for arranging the blood for the patients they can contact us by our email by our social media or by simply contact us on the call. we will provide the blood arrangement as soon as possible
